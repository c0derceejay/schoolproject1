About Me

Hello, my name is Chris Jackson. I am 30 years old and a Freelance Web Developer pursuing my Bachelors degree in Information Technology. I am a Father to a 2 year old daughter and she is my world, always keeping me on my toes and in tune with the latest Disney movie or character. I have been serving Active Duty in the US Army for the past 11 years now, and have decided to pursue my dreams as a Developer after my current contract ends. For now I have been spending my time learning as much as I can about Web Development and more so Python programming. My web development experience comes from the last 2 years of completing online bootcamps and following along Youtube tutorials and I have built a solid foundation with those resources.
